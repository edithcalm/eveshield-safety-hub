About EveShield

EveShield is a non-profit organization dedicated to building an integrated safety ecosystem for survivors of gender-based violence and sexual assault in Kenya. Our mission is to empower individuals with instant emergency response, real-time location sharing, and access to survivor-centered support for long-term healing and justice.
